How to play Traps'n'Thrones

SinglePlayer

  1. Click on a banner
  2. Select your character
  3. Click Start Game button

Multiplayer(local)

  1. Click on a banner
  2. Select your character
  3. Click on a banner
  4. Select your second character
  5. Click Start Game button

Rules

The sules are simple.

  1. Roll the dice
  2. Wait for your token to move
  3. The first Player to reach the end of the board WINS!

But watch out for traps
